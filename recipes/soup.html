Homemade Chicken Soup

You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken
  • 4 carrots, halved
  • 4 stalks celery, halved
  • 1 large onion, halved
  • water to cover
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules

Steps

  1. Place chicken, carrots, celery, and onion in a large soup pot; add enough cold water to cover.
  2. Remove chicken from the pot and let sit until cool enough to handle; chop meat into pieces, and discard skin and bones.
  3. Strain out vegetables, reserving the stock; rinse the soup pot and return the stock to the pot.
  4. Warm soup until heated through; season with salt, pepper, and chicken bouillon to taste.
